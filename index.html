Auto-Copy Selection

Stop reaching for Ctrl+C. Just highlight any text on any webpage or click a cell in Google Sheets, and it's instantly copied to your clipboard.

Clipboard History

Never lose a copied link again. Simply right-click anywhere to bring up your last 15 copied items in a sleek, non-intrusive custom menu.

Rapid Alt+Click Paste

Hold the Alt key and click inside any input field, text area, or rich text editor to instantly paste your most recent clipboard content.

Customizable Feedback

Get satisfying visual confirmation ("Copied!" / "Pasted!") right where your cursor is. Customize colors to match your aesthetic in the popup settings.

100% Secure & Private

Your clipboard history never leaves your device. Data is stored purely in local Chrome storage. No external servers or analytics tracking.

Easy Master Switch

Need native browser shortcuts back for a moment? Instantly toggle the extension on or off with a single switch from the toolbar popup.
