Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

M Nadeem Akhtar ("Developer", "we", "our", or "us") operates the Smart Clipboard Chrome Extension (the "Extension"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Extension.

We believe in absolute privacy. Your data belongs to you.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers.

The core functionality of the Smart Clipboard extension requires intercepting the text you select on web pages and saving it to your device's clipboard. This action is performed entirely locally within your browser environment.

2. Chrome Local Storage & Permissions

To provide features like the "Clipboard History" custom menu and user preferences (like visual feedback colors), the Extension utilizes the `chrome.storage` API.

  • Clipboard History: A history of your copied items is saved locally on your device storage. This data never leaves your computer. You can clear this history at any time from the Extension's settings popup.
  • Permissions Explained:
    • clipboardWrite: Required to automate the action of copying selected text directly to your system clipboard.
    • storage: Required to save your clipboard history and settings locally.
    • scripting: Required to seamlessly update the extension's behavior across active tabs without forcing you to refresh your web pages.
    • <all_urls>: Required so the auto-copy and paste functionalities work correctly across all websites you visit.

3. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools within the Extension. We do not track the websites you visit or the content you copy.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any information to outside parties because we do not collect any information in the first place.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support tab or email me directly at nadeem@mastertech.com (example).
